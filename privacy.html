Sovrano Gourmet – Privacy Policy Last updated: February 28, 2026 Sovrano Gourmet (“we”, “our”, “the app”) is a mobile application designed for internal restaurant operations, including inventory management, station workflows, and user authentication. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. 1. Information We Collect 1.1 Account Information We collect the following information when you sign in or access a station: Email address Station identifier (e.g., estacion_1, estacion_2, etc.) Role (usuario, invitado) 1.2 Device Information We may collect basic device information such as: Device model Operating system version App version Crash logs (for debugging) 1.3 Camera Access The app uses the device camera only to scan QR codes for station login. We do not record, store, or transmit camera images or video. 2. How We Use Your Information We use collected information to: Authenticate users Assign the correct station Manage inventory and operational workflows Improve app performance and stability Maintain security and prevent unauthorized access We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties. 3. Data Storage and Security All data is stored securely using Google Firebase services. We use: Firestore Firebase Authentication Firebase Storage (if applicable) Firebase may store data in multiple regions to ensure reliability and performance. We implement industry‑standard security practices, including: Encrypted connections (HTTPS) Access control rules Role‑based permissions 4. Children’s Privacy Sovrano Gourmet is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. 5. Third‑Party Services The app uses the following third‑party services: Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Storage) Expo (camera module and runtime environment) These services may collect diagnostic or usage data as described in their respective privacy policies. 6. Your Choices You may request: Deletion of your account Correction of your information Access to your stored data To make a request, contact us at: support@sovranogourmet.com 7. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at: https://sovrano.app/privacy 8.
