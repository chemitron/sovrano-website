Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Sovrano Gourmet (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates the Sovrano Gourmet mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

The Sovrano Gourmet app does not collect personal information from customers. The app is used internally by authorized staff members to manage orders, inventory, and station workflows.

How We Use Information

Any operational data entered by staff is used solely for restaurant operations, including:

  • Managing orders
  • Tracking inventory
  • Authenticating staff stations

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or disclose any information to third parties. All data remains internal to Sovrano Gourmet.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect operational data, including secure authentication and restricted access.

Children’s Privacy

The app is not intended for consumer use and is not directed toward children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
